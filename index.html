Abstract

We present a method for photorealistically reconstructing non-rigidly deforming scenes from casually captured videos.

Our approach augments neural radiance fields with a continuous volumetric deformation field and introduces a coarse-to-fine optimization scheme to improve robustness. The result enables rendering realistic views of dynamic scenes.
